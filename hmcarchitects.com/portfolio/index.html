Civic

Designed to bring people together around shared interests and community purpose, our civic projects aim to express the needs and aspirations of communities, foster community involvement, and help civic organizations better serve the public.

Healthcare

Wellness is paramount to a thriving community. We’ve spent decades focused on planning, researching, and designing healthcare facilities that streamline processes, eliminate waste, and promote health and well-being for both patients and clinical staff. Experts in applying Lean principles to complex projects, we’re recognized leaders in implementing successful projects through collaborative, contractor-integrated delivery methods.

Download Healthcare PDF Brochure

Higher Education

From residence halls to libraries and beyond, we work with campus communities of all sizes, scales, and cultures to develop design solutions that not only inspire imagination and creativity, but also unite students, teachers, and the broader community to create a space that feels inclusive, organic, and energized.

Download Higher Education PDF Brochure

PreK-12

Great schools benefit the whole community. That’s why we aim to create future-focused, healthy, resilient schools that embrace discovery, accommodate various learning styles, and inspire students and teachers to succeed.

Download PreK-12 PDF Brochure

Sustainability

Sustainability is in our DNA. We believe that buildings should positively impact the environment and benefit society while also promoting human health and quality of life. We work internally as well as with our clients to measure, plan, and reduce our carbon footprint with the overarching goal of zero carbon architecture.

Download Sustainability PDF Brochure
